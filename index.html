Clax

Clax is a strategy puzzle game in which you remove groups of colored balls in a way that allows you to clear the board of all every ball.

Clax has 6 difficulties, 2 ball sizes and a system to track and view your high scores.

Be cautioned, this game is extremely addicting. I'm sure my wife has logged more hours playing this game than she has driving our car.

BCD Clock

Whether you want to impress your alpha friends or just confuse all of your normal buddies, a binary clock is a sure fire way to achieve your goal.

As you may have guessed, this simple clock uses binary to display the current time down to the second, with a hypnotic tick-tock sound to back to grey and blue dots. No preferences, no frills. Great utility to satisfy the geek in all of us!
